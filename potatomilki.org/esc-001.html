ESC-001 “Hostile Spam”

Object Class (Euclid)

Description:

Spam is a highly fragile and heavily radioactive tinned piece of meat normally made from ham and it currently stands (As Of ███) with ESC-004, if any living organism touches spam or gets thrown at by ESC-004 you get sent to the spam dimension, this dimension is positioned in between the moon and earth, it moves with the rotation on earth, and it is impossible to live there without any protective gear, hazmat suit, etc.

Special Containment Procedures:

It sits in a room in the light containment zone of site 420, guarding it is ESC-423 mostly to scare off scientists trying to steal/take spam for unknown reasons.

Origins:

This was never supposed to hurt anyone, it was actually to help people, it was made in a laboratory approximately ████ years ago it still has some healing side effects like curing cancer and common colds, but it will send you to the spam realm, there are still non-toxic pieces of it in site 420 called ESC-500 read more on that in another file in this folder.
